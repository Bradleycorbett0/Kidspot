Welcome to KIDSPOT

KIDSPOT helps families and youth workers find free and affordable activities for children aged 10–16 — instantly. Whether it’s football in a church hall, coding classes at a library, or creative arts in a youth club, KIDSPOT maps and filters it all in one place.

Why It Matters

  • Millions of families don’t know what’s available locally.
  • Youth workers struggle to keep up with scattered event info.
  • Young people miss out — not due to money, but missing information.

Built by Lived Experience

This platform was born from real struggle — homelessness, poverty, and frontline youth work. It’s not built for clicks — it’s built to connect.
